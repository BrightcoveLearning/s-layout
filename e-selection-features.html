Introduction

You can choose the player used in your Beacon apps. Player E is the latest player, and eventually all new customers automatically get Player E. Also, eventually all existing customers will move to Player E.

Player E is a player for VOD videos, linear channels and live events in which viewers will watch media assets. The player incorporates many new features across all platforms. This topic will detail features available, and not available, across all platforms. A demonstration of Player E's features is also available.

Player E is used in multiple locations in your Beacon apps. In Beacon web apps, all Player E instances are based on Brightcove Player. For more information on the instances and what Brightcove Player plugins are used for each instance, see the Brightcove Player Plugin Compatibility document.

VOD Player selection

To select and configure your player, follow these steps:

  1. In the primary navigation, click Layout.
  2. Click Settings .

  3. The App Settings page appears. In Assets, click Videos to start.

  4. Click Player to see how the player displays information about the currently playing video.
  5. The different information layouts for the videos are displayed by letters (A,E). Note that as you click on the different layout, the preview changes to display your layout and options.
    Warning: Once your select Player E, you will only see configuration options for Player E and will no longer be able to select Player A.

Episodic content Player selection

To select and configure your player, follow these steps:

  1. In the primary navigation, click Layout.
  2. Click Settings .
  3. The App Settings page appears. In Assets, click Episodic content to start.
  4. Click Player to see how the player displays information about the currently playing video.
  5. The different information layouts for the videos are displayed by letters (A,E). Note that as you click on the different layout, the preview changes to display your layout and options.
    Warning: Once your select Player E, you will only see configuration options for Player E and will no longer be able to select Player A.

Live events Player selection

  1. In the primary navigation, click Layout.
  2. Click Settings .
  3. The App Settings page appears. In Assets, click Live events to start.
