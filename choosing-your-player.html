Introduction

Player E is a player for VOD videos and Live video streams in which viewers will watch media assets. This player version was released near the end of 2022. All customers that came on board after Player E release will use Player E and existing customers will move to Player E. The player incorporates many new features across all platforms. This topic will detail features available, and not available, across all platforms. A demonstration of Player E's features is also available.
